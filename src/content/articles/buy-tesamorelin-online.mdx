## Prescription Tesamorelin And Research Tesamorelin Are Different

The prescription product context is EGRIFTA SV, which has a label, a defined indication, specific reconstitution instructions, and clinician oversight. Online research tesamorelin listings are a separate category. They are usually lyophilized research peptide vials sold by research vendors.

That distinction matters because EGRIFTA SV is tied to a specific indication: reduction of excess abdominal fat in adults with HIV and lipodystrophy. Research-vendor listings do not become prescription EGRIFTA SV because they use the tesamorelin name. They need to stand on their own product clarity, vendor reputation, and documentation.

## Why Tesamorelin Deserves A Higher Buying Standard

Tesamorelin has more clinical support than many compounds in the peptide market. A major randomized trial reported a 10.9% visceral adipose tissue reduction at six months with tesamorelin compared with 0.6% with placebo. Longer follow-up reported sustained visceral-fat reduction with continued treatment, and other research has measured liver-fat changes as well.

That is a strong clinical story. It does not make every online product high quality. It makes product quality more important. When the research topic is serious, vague listings are a problem. The vendor should make it easy to understand what is being sold, how it is documented, and how other buyers have reviewed the vendor.
