## GHK-Cu Serum Is Not The Same Product

Before comparing vendors, make sure the product format is clear. GHK-Cu serum and GHK-Cu cream are topical cosmetic products. GHK-Cu peptide powder is a research peptide product, usually sold as a lyophilized vial.

Those categories can share the same core ingredient concept, but they do not serve the same buying intent. Someone looking for a face serum should not accidentally buy a research vial, and someone comparing research peptide vendors should not treat a cosmetic product listing like a COA-backed peptide listing.
