## COAs And Verification

COA visibility should be near the top of the checklist. It does not prove everything about a vendor, but it shows whether the listing is supported by testing information.

Ratings, review count, shipping clarity, payment options, and product detail help complete the picture. A vendor does not need to be perfect, but the listing should give enough information to make a fair comparison.

For retatrutide specifically, COA review should be product-specific. A vendor-level claim that "testing is available" is less useful than a retatrutide listing that shows current testing context for the compound being compared.

## Payment Methods And Shipping Options

Payment support can decide which vendors are practical. Some vendors list credit card support, some list ACH, and others support crypto. Shipping is similar: fast shipping and international shipping can matter as much as the list price.

For "near me" searches, shipping and country details are usually more useful than physical location. The real question is whether the vendor ships to the relevant region and makes those logistics clear.

That is why local availability should be interpreted carefully. A nearby vendor is not automatically better than a vendor with clearer COAs, better payment support, stronger reviews, and reliable shipping.
