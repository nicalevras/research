## Quick Answer

Tesamorelin is interesting to bodybuilding readers because it has clinical evidence around visceral abdominal fat and waist-profile change. That makes it especially relevant to physique goals where the goal is a tighter midsection rather than simple scale-weight loss.

The strongest dosage sources are not bodybuilding forums. They are the EGRIFTA SV label and clinical trials. The current EGRIFTA SV label lists 1.4 mg once daily, while major studies with the older formulation commonly used 2 mg subcutaneously once daily. Those numbers explain the evidence base, not a personal bodybuilding protocol.

## Why Bodybuilders Pay Attention To Tesamorelin

Tesamorelin is not famous because it is a direct anabolic steroid or a stimulant. It is famous because it targets a body-composition problem that many physique-focused readers care about: abdominal fat that does not behave like easy subcutaneous fat.

In the major randomized trial, tesamorelin reduced visceral adipose tissue by 10.9% at six months compared with 0.6% with placebo. Waist circumference, waist-to-hip ratio, and belly-profile ratings also improved. That is exactly why the bodybuilding conversation exists.

## Not A Classic Muscle-Building Drug

Tesamorelin should not be sold as a shortcut for muscle gain. Its best evidence is not bench-press performance, hypertrophy, or strength. Its best evidence is visceral-fat reduction in a specific clinical population.

That still matters for physique goals. A smaller waist and improved abdominal profile can dramatically change how lean someone looks, even when the scale does not move like it would with a broad weight-loss drug.

## Dosage Context For Physique Readers

The current EGRIFTA SV label lists 1.4 mg once daily. Older EGRIFTA clinical trials commonly studied 2 mg once daily. DailyMed explains that systemic exposure is similar between the 1.4 mg EGRIFTA SV dose and the older 2 mg EGRIFTA dose.

This matters because bodybuilding content often copies numbers without explaining formulation. Tesamorelin dosage should always be tied to the source, formulation, and goal being discussed.

## Cycle Thinking

Bodybuilding readers often think in cycles. Tesamorelin research is better understood through clinical-study timelines. The key studies measured outcomes at 26 weeks, and extension data looked at continued use over 52 weeks.

That does not mean every physique reader is following a 26-week protocol. It means the visible body-composition story is not a two-week event. Tesamorelin is a slower, abdominal-body-composition peptide.

## Men, Women, And Expectations

The label dose is not split into separate male and female doses. Trials included waist and waist-to-hip inclusion criteria that differed by sex, but the core dose language was not a casual men-versus-women gym protocol.

For physique readers, the most realistic expectation is gradual abdominal-profile improvement when visceral fat is the relevant issue. Tesamorelin is less convincing if the goal is rapid scale loss, extreme appetite suppression, or direct muscle gain.
